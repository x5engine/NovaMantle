## üèÜ How We Hit Every Track

### 1. üè¢ RWA / RealFi (Primary Track)
* **Core Feature:** One-click factory that converts PDF Invoices/Deeds into **ERC-1155 Yield-Bearing Tokens**.
* **Yield:** Idle assets are automatically swapped to **Ondo USDY** to generate passive income.

### 2. üéÆ GameFi & Social
* **The Hook:** "Finance as Gameplay."
* **Mechanics:** Users earn **XP** for originating assets ("Scouting"). The "Horizontal Ticker" creates a sense of a multiplayer live market.
* **VFX:** High-fidelity sound effects (SFX) and particle animations make minting an invoice feel like "crafting a legendary item."

### 3. ü§ñ AI & Oracles
* **The Brain:** Our Python AI Agent scans uploaded PDFs.
* **The Oracle:** It assigns a **Risk Score (0-100)** and pushes a signed price verification to the blockchain via **RedStone Oracles**.

### 4. üîí ZK & Privacy
* **The Shield:** ZK-Proofs allow users to prove "Asset Ownership" without doxxing the physical address of the real estate or the name on the invoice.

### 5. üèó Infrastructure & Mantle Integration
* **The Backbone:** We use **Mantle DA (EigenDA)** to store the heavy "Proof of Asset" (PDFs, Legal Docs, AI Reports).
* **Why:** This saves ~90% on gas compared to on-chain storage and prevents "Asset Proof of Bullshit."

### 6. üé® Best UX / Demo
* **The Feel:** No loading spinners. We use **Optimistic UI**, **Sound Design (use-sound)**, and **Framer Motion** for a buttery-smooth, 60fps "Control Room" experience.
